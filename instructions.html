1. Select Your Seats: Click on the interactive seating plan to choose your preferred seats. Seats that are available will be highlighted.

  2. Information: Fill in your personal information:
    Name: Enter your full name.
    Email: Provide a valid email address.
    Phone Number: Include a reachable phone number.
    Number of Tickets: Indicate the number of tickets you want to purchase. The pricing will be updated based on your selection.

  3. Pricing Details: Review the pricing details to ensure accuracy, including the total cost of your selected tickets.

  4. Additional Services (Optional): You have the option to add extra services to enhance your concert experience. Select any additional services you'd like.

  5. Proceed to Payment: Click the "Proceed to Payment" button to move to the payment page. You will be redirected to a secure payment gateway.

  6. Payment: Enter your payment information, including credit card details or other payment methods. Ensure that you receive a payment confirmation.

  7. Confirmation: After successful payment, you will receive a booking confirmation via email. Keep this confirmation for your records.

  8. Arrival: On the day of the concert, arrive at the venue with your confirmation email and valid ID to collect your tickets. Enjoy the show!
